White people tacos

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Flour tortias
  • Mild Salsa
  • Taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat ground beef on large skillet

    Cook and stir for 5-7 minutes until browned

  2. Strain grease from ground beef

    Stir in taco seasoning

  3. Lay taco shell on plate

    Add ground beef, shredded cheese, and salsa. Enjoy!
