Baking tray pizza

Ingredients

  • Store bought pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Get dough ready

    Pat out the dough into rectangle baking tray, add cheese, sauce, and pepperoni

  2. Preheat the oven to 475

    Put the pizza in the oven and wait 10 to 15 min

  3. Let cool for 5 minutes

    Take it out and enjoy
